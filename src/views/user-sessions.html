About Sessions
Session Status Types:
  • Current Your current active session
  • Active Other valid login sessions
  • Expired Sessions that have timed out
  • Revoked Sessions you've manually ended
Security Tips:
  • Review sessions regularly
  • Revoke unrecognized sessions immediately
  • Use "Revoke All" when changing passwords
  • Log out from public/shared devices
